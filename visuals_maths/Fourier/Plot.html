Visualisation Guidance

This is the guidance for the Fourier visualisation Plot.
It provides you the plot of the Fourier Series as you change the amplitude and the period of the function, and the number of terms of the series.

You can change the function you want from the scroll down

Amplitude Slider allows you to change the amplitude of the function

Number of Terms Slider allows you to increase or decrease the number of Fourier Series terms

Period Slider allows you to change the period of the function

Have fun!
