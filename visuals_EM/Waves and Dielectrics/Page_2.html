2. Snell's Law

Next all we have to do is incorporate Snell's Law of refraction. This law states that $$\frac{sin(\theta_{i})}{sin(\theta_{t})} = \frac{n_{t}}{n_{i}} $$ From this we can see that given a certain refractive index ratio, the outgoing direction of a wave can be determined if the incoming direction is known.
